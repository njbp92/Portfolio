SPU’s Tuition Assistance Program (TAP) provides financial assistance to eligible employees interested in furthering their education or advancing their skills in support of continued professional development.

Qualified SPU employees are limited to a lifetime maximum of $14,000. These funds are awarded annually for up to $3,500 in total expenses.

TAP is open to part-time or full-time SPU employees who have been regular status employees for one (1) full year at SPU.


Employees must submit a completed TAP Application.

If the link is disabled or prefer a PDF application, submit a completed TAP Application PDF to spu_training@seattle.gov.

Allowable expenditures include, but are not limited to:

  • tuition from accredited degree programs
  • certificate programs
  • books
