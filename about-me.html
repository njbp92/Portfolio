Backstory

Back during the myspace times, I dabbled in basic html coding without really thinking it was coding a language. Little did I know, I would be teaching myself how to code to maintain websites for my previous employer. I had spent tons of time on youtube, google and asking around to get as much information as possible to figure things out, but that wasn't enough. There was always a piece missing. Enrolling into the UW Certificate program was the first step to grow mys kills as a coder and finally have a stronger foundation for what I had just happened to pick up on my own.
